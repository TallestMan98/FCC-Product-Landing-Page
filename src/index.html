Premium Materials

Our trombones use only the highest quality brass, which undergoes stringent mechanical testing to ensure reliability of our products. Our brass is formed from a unique blend of copper and zinc, which provides resistance to weathering and all manners of wear and tear. Each trombone is made by hand by our master brass-smiths. We are so confident in the quality of our trombones that each one comes with a life-long guarantee!

Fast Shipping

We make good use of our Great British Royal Mail service. Customers can choose between First Class and Second Class delivery options depending on how desperate you are to get a grasp of your brass. Each trombone is made to order and we will ship the order as soon as it is complete. We also offer free return shipping if you are unsatisfied in any way with your trombone.

Quality Assurance

Every instrument we produce undergoes rigorous quality assurance testing and comes pre-pitched. Our trombones are delivered fully constructed in dark oak cases with a rich velvet padding. Our instruments are fit for purpose straight from the box, whether this is taking centre-stage in a full orchestra or playing along to some slow, solemn blues at home.
